congestion pricing eliminated in final four

April 9, 2013

There was a March Madness competition among urbanist policies over at Atlantic Cities, and congestion pricing made it all the way to the Final Four but lost out to bike lanes. Great job guys, and better luck next year. I don’t think congestion pricing would have made it out of the first round five years ago. It’s a strong program that can only get better, and we’ve got some great prospects coming up.
