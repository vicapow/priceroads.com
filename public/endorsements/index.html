Endorsements

Here's a list of prominent people and organizations who have endorsed road pricing. Currently, I have no women on the list. Please email me prominent women who support road pricing.

Researchers

  • Dr. William Vickrey – Winner of the Nobel Memorial Prize in Economics
  • Dr. Milton Friedman – Winner of the Nobel Memorial Prize in Economics
  • Dr. Daniel McFadden – Winner of the Nobel Memorial Prize in Economics
  • Dr. Walter Block – Senior Fellow at the von Mises Institute
  • Randall O’Toole – Senior Fellow at the Cato Institute
  • Dr. Ronald Utt – Senior Research Fellow at the Heritage Foundation
  • Dr. Kenneth Small – Professor of Economics at UC-Irvine, author of Urban Transportation Economics
  • Dr. Greg Mankiw – Professor of Economics at Harvard University, Chairman of the Council of Economics Advisors under George W. Bush
  • Dr. Paul Romer – Professor of Economics at NYU Stern, charter cities
  • Dr. Ed Glaeser – author of Triump of the City and Professor of Economics at Harvard University
  • Robert Puentes – Senior Fellow, Brookings Metropolitan Policy Program
  • Robert Poole – Director of Transportation Policy at the Reason Foundation
  • Dr. Nancy Folbre – Professor of Economics at UMass Amherst

Politicians

  • George W. Bush
  • Ken Livingstone – former Mayor of London
  • Michael Bloomberg – Mayor of New York City

Bloggers

  • Dr. Tyler Cowen – Marginal Revolution/GMU econ professor
  • Dr. Scott Sumner – The Money Illusion/Bentley U. econ professor
  • Streetsblog – National Livable Streets Blogging Network
  • Transportation Alternatives – advocacy group for walking, bicycling, and public transit
  • The Infrastructurist – Infrastructure advocacy blog
  • Matt Yglesias
  • Ryan Avent
  • Reihan Salam
